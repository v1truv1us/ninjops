# Business Brochure Site Example

This examples use the `biz_quote.json` to demonstrate a typical business quote with standard features.

- Professional presentation with business terminology
- Pricing structure for multiple line items
- Optional deposit

- Project details and timeline

- Optional terms/conditions

- optional AI assistance
- Compatible with Invoice Ninja v5

Business brochure websites are essential for:
 consulting business to reach new audiences. This example creates a **5-page brochure website** for a local construction company using a simple templating and Invoice Ninja integration.
